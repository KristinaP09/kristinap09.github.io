1. Federated Multi-View K-Means with Enhanced Distance (Fed-MVKM-ED)

Comprehensive Implementation & Publication Demonstration
Developed and demonstrated a complete federated multi-view clustering algorithm for privacy-preserving distributed learning. The implementation features rectified Gaussian kernels, differential privacy mechanisms, and adaptive view weight learning.

Key Technical Achievements:

  • Performance: NMI: 0.8925, ARI: 0.6999 (32.7% improvement over local models)
  • Privacy-Preserving: Differential privacy with 0.9 privacy level
  • Multi-View Integration: Depth (6144D) + RGB (110D) feature fusion
  • Federated Setup: 3-site collaboration (Research Hospital, Rehab Center, Sports Clinic)
  • Comprehensive Evaluation: 12+ visualizations, convergence analysis, performance metrics
Dataset: DHA (Depth-included Human Action) simulation with 23 action categories across 600 samples. Applications: Healthcare collaboration, IoT networks, multi-institutional research.

Complete implementation available in Comprehensive_demonstration.ipynb with publication-ready results and analysis.
