Dealing with Stupidity Everywhere: My Blunt Take

No sugar-coating. Just real talk about stupidity and professionalism.

September 23, 2025

Forget Arguing with Stupid

I don’t waste my breath arguing with stupid anymore. And no, I’m not just talking about people without degrees. You’ll find stupidity everywhere—even in so-called professionals adored by clueless crowds, and students from both elite and terrible universities. Some shout their ignorance loud and proud. Others? Silent but deadly dull.

The only way to stay sane is to walk away and let them take the spotlight. Sure, they think they’ve won when you stop responding. What they don’t know is you’ve simply run out of patience. You can’t fix stupid. It’s permanent, and it doesn’t care where it lives—whether a developing or developed country.

Degrees Don't Mean Jack

The worst are those with fancy degrees but zero professionalism. They have all the privileges yet spread stupidity like it’s a rare breed they’re proud of. I worked at top universities and research centers—from Taiwan to Italy—and honestly, "culture" sometimes means a whole lot of talking with zero action.

People pretend to collaborate while secretly hating each other, all for a paycheck. They show up but accomplish little. Some even consult monks or paranormal experts for jobs or peace of mind. If those folks actually had power, why aren’t they fixing the country instead of giving nonsense talks?

The Public vs. Private Circus

Public institutions at least have transparency. Private ones? They operate like shady apps running stealth mode in the background. Therapists and other pros sometimes back these unprofessional "professionals" and add to the stupidity parade.

Degrees can be just fancy labels for people who contribute almost nothing and cause plenty of frustration.

Italy: Developed but a Mess

Italy, of all places, shocked me. A country with ancient Rome and Ferrari but with people who don’t clean toilets properly or feel the need to brag with sticky notes about the bare minimum they did. Some clowns with degrees act like royalty, completely clueless that I’m silently judging them as the most disgusting humans I’ve met.

Big institutions brag about contracts and brands but fail at basics—like not providing accommodation or a simple signed certificate to a visiting researcher. How can you be proud of Ferrari when your systems are broken?

They’ll Never Impress Me Again

I used to admire graduates from Western and Eastern elites. Now? They’re just hired clowns doing the bare minimum to survive. No impact, just nonsense. But I’m not done exploring. I’m here for both the stupidity and the rare flashes of knowledge.

To those hoping I’ll fail: keep waiting. You won’t see it happen. One day, you might even end up working with me—karma’s a real thing.

"You’ll never win an argument with stupid, but you can always choose not to play their game."

Still Standing, Still Exploring

I might have a headache from grinding my teeth, but my mind’s clear: stupidity is everywhere and everywhere it stinks. But I’m not backing down. Resilience and curiosity keep me going. Ready for the next mess and lesson.
