Your Time Is Not My Time: A Public Service Announcement for the Clown Network

Let’s get this one thing straight—your time is not my time.

Just because your circus of stupidity starts spinning its wheels at 6 AM doesn’t mean I’m part of it. I sleep when I want. I work when it makes sense. I create on my schedule, not yours.

To the upstairs chair-draggers, the downstairs signal droppers, and the whispering puppet squad: Stop syncing your clown show to my sleep cycle. I didn’t buy a ticket.

Dear Stupid, You’re Out of Sync

Here’s what you don’t get: not everyone is operating on your broken corporate clock. Some of us—shockingly—think for ourselves. Some of us have outgrown 9-to-5 mental prisons and don’t feel the need to perform “awake” just to please your surveillance schedule.

I went to sleep at 5 AM. Not your concern. Not your invitation.

“You disturb me while I sleep, and you call that therapy? That’s not healing. That’s harassment in disguise.”

Dragging Your Nonsense Into My Dreams? Try Harder

Yes, I woke up from your ridiculous narratives. Yes, I noticed the Bluetooth tricks and the earbuds disconnections. And yes, your chair-dragging signature move? Still pathetic.

But you know what I felt most? Disgust. You’re still acting like it’s 2021. Still recycling the same copy-paste clown behaviors from 2022 and pretending it’s a brand-new circus.

Your outdated stupidity is not a revival—it's a rerun no one asked for.

Final Note to Your Puppets

I see your cheap parrots and puppets. The ones you trained to walk like humans but talk like scripts. They can whisper my name, mimic my preferences, and try to guilt trip me using family drama from three years ago. But guess what? That script has expired. And no—I’m not buying your redemption arc.

If they touch my drink in a dream, it’s symbolic. If they try it in reality, it’s war. And no—your stupid birds chirping won’t save you.

The Standard Is Above the Clouds

My time, my space, my rules. I don’t wake up for clowns, I don’t react to manipulation, and I sure as hell don’t sleep under anyone’s control. My standard? Above the clouds. Yours? Still scraping the dirt with broken logic and fake therapy sessions.

“Keep trying. Your time’s ticking. Mine’s already timeless.”
